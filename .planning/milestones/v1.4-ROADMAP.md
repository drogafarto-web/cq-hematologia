# v1.4 ROADMAP

**Strategic North Star:** Complete RDC 978 compliance (end-to-end), close the DICQ gap to 88–92% (from 78–82%), launch patient-facing portals (external feedback loop + laudo download), and establish the IA foundation for strip OCR imuno classification.

**Duration:** 22 weeks (2026-05-07 → 2026-09-30)  
**Parallel Streams:** 4 (Wave-based delivery with strict dependencies)  
**Success Criteria:** DICQ ≥88%, RDC 978 100%, zero P0 security findings, <5% churn, NPS >50.

---

## Vision & Strategic Context

v1.3 delivered the **foundation** — 25 modules in production, ~78–82% DICQ baseline, 12 CAPAs closed by infrastructure. v1.4 closes the **operational gaps**: customer portals (laudo download, NPS feedback), RDC 978 Part 2 (NOTIVISA, critical value escalation, documentation), and launches the **IA leap** (imuno strip OCR classification).

**Key constraints:**

- Must reach ≥88% DICQ before external audit (target: Oct 2026)
- RDC 978 100% coverage required for ANVISA regulatory standing
- Multi-tenant architecture remains single-lab (Opção A decision) — v1.5 will expand
- No breaking changes to v1.3 schema or auth model

**Teams/Streams:** Assume 4 parallel agents (Streams A–D) with staggered start:

- **Stream A (CAPA Closure & DICQ Blocks):** Sequential (depends on auditor availability)
- **Stream B (Portal Expansion & RDC 978 Part 2):** Parallel from Week 1
- **Stream C (IA Foundation & Imuno Prep):** Parallel from Week 3
- **Stream D (Performance & DevOps):** Continuous from Week 1
