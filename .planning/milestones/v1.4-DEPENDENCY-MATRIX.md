## Wave-Based Parallelization Strategy

### Wave 1 (Weeks 1–3): Serial Foundation

**Goal:** Unblock all downstream work.  
**Phases:** 1 → 2 → 3  
**Duration:** 3 weeks (zero flex)  
**Outcome:** Schema live, requirements locked, all streams ready.

### Wave 2 (Weeks 4–8): 3-Stream Parallel

**Goal:** Close CAPA, launch portals, start escalation.  
**Streams:**
- **Stream A:** Phase 4 (CAPA) — 20 days, auditor-dependent
- **Stream B:** Phases 5, 6, 7 (Portal + Críticos + Satisfação) — 17 days max
- **Stream D:** Smoke gates + cost monitoring — continuous

**Duration:** 5 weeks  
**Outcome:** DICQ 78–82% → 86%, RDC 978 Arts. 184–191 complete, portals live.  
**Parallelization factor:** 3 streams × 5 weeks = 15 stream-weeks (vs. 10 serial weeks without overlap).

### Wave 3 (Weeks 9–15): 4-Stream Parallel

**Goal:** Complete compliance + IA foundation.  
**Streams:**
- **Stream A:** Phases 8, 9 (NOTIVISA + Docs) — 24 days
- **Stream B:** Phase 10 (CIQ multi) — 17 days
- **Stream C:** Phase 11 (IA) — 14 days (stagger Week 12)
- **Stream D:** Continuous (Lighthouse CI + cost)

**Duration:** 7 weeks  
**Outcome:** DICQ 86% → 88%, RDC 978 100%, IA dataset >500 images, multi-equipment validated.  
**Parallelization factor:** 4 streams × 7 weeks = 28 stream-weeks (vs. 24 serial weeks).

### Wave 4 (Weeks 16–22): Serial Finish (Quality Gate)

**Goal:** Audit, security-check, deploy.  
**Phases:** 12 → 13 → 14 → 15  
**Duration:** 7 weeks (zero flex on critical path)  
**Outcome:** v1.4 live, ≥88% DICQ, 100% RDC 978, production-ready.

## Contingency Plans

### If Phase 4 (CAPA) slips >1 week:

- **Option A:** Compress Phases 5–7 to 3 weeks (high risk, not recommended)
- **Option B:** Continue Phases 5–7 in parallel; extend launch by 1 week (acceptable)
- **Option C:** CTO escalation to auditor (request expedited RFI response)

**Impact:** Phase 15 launch shifts from Week 20 → Week 21 (1-week slip).

### If Phase 12 (Perf Audit) finds major regression:

- **Option A:** Identify commit causing regression; fix immediately (3–5 days)
- **Option B:** Disable feature causing regression; defer to v1.4.1
- **Option C:** Accept performance miss (not recommended; breaches CLAUDE.md standard)

**Impact:** Phase 15 launch shifts from Week 20 → Week 22 (2-week slip).

### If Phase 13 (DICQ Audit) finds >5 P0 gaps:

- **Option A:** Extend Phase 14 by 1 week; fix gaps immediately (2–3 days work)
- **Option B:** Defer P0 gaps to v1.4.1 (with auditor acceptance)
- **Option C:** CTO review; escalate for director decision

**Impact:** Phase 15 launch shifts from Week 20 → Week 21 (1-week slip).

### If Phase 14 (Security Audit) finds high-severity finding:

- **Option A:** Fix immediately (1–3 days)
- **Option B:** Document exception + CTO sign-off (only if finding is environmental, not code)
- **Option C:** Defer to v1.4.1 (blocks v1.4 launch)

**Impact:** If Option A, Phase 15 becomes Week 21. If Option B, Phase 15 is Week 20. If Option C, v1.4 delayed to v1.4.1 (2+ weeks).
