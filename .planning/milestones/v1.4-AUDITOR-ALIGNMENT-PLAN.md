## Call Agenda (90 min)

### Part 1 — v1.3 Status Review (20 min)

**Objective**: ground the auditor on what is already in production.

- v1.3 deployment summary: 25 modules, DICQ 78.5% coverage
- Smoke tests results (Step 4 execution log)
- Open compliance items carried into v1.4
- Live demo: audit trail event chain (chainHash) + signed laudo PDF

**Output**: auditor acknowledges v1.3 as baseline.

### Part 2 — v1.4 Plan Walkthrough (30 min)

**Objective**: get directional approval on v1.4 scope.

- 4 RDC blockers in Phase 0:
  - Supervisor técnico (RT) signature flows
  - LGPD direitos do titular operationalization
  - Laboratórios de apoio contracts + traceability
  - Risk management (ISO 14971-style FMEA)
- 22-week roadmap overview (Phases 0 → 6)
- DICQ 78.5% → 85%+ trajectory by block (A–J)
- Mandatory dates: **2026-08-15 audit prep target**
- Highlight deferred items (what is NOT in v1.4 and why)

**Output**: auditor confirms scope is sufficient OR flags gaps before Phase 0.

### Part 3 — RFI Cadence Agreement (20 min)

**Objective**: lock the operational protocol for the next 22 weeks.

- Async RFI submission channel (preferred: shared SGD folder + email notification)
- **Auditor response SLA: target 5 business days**
- Escalation path if response > SLA (CTO direct call)
- Batch frequency: weekly RFI digest vs. ad-hoc per item
- RFI template fields: requirement ID, evidence requested, decision needed, deadline

**Output**: written RFI protocol confirmed by auditor (email or signed minute).

### Part 4 — Evidence Standards (15 min)

**Objective**: avoid Phase 4 rework.

- Audit trail format: is `LogicalSignature { hash, operatorId, ts }` + chainHash acceptable as digital signature equivalent for DICQ 4.4 / RDC 978?
- Document versioning conventions: SGD semver vs. auditor-expected scheme
- Raw data access: Firestore export procedure + retention guarantees
- PDF report layout expectations (FR-\* forms — physical Labclin layout already in prod)
- Backup + retention policy alignment (5 years minimum per RDC 978)

**Output**: auditor signs off on evidence format OR specifies required adjustments before Phase 1.

### Part 5 — Q&A + Action Items (5 min)

- Capture open questions
- Confirm next checkpoint date (end of Wave 1, Week 3)
- Confirm comms channel + SLA in writing
