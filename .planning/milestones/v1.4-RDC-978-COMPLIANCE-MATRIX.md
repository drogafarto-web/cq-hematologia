### Risk of Future ANVISA Guidance Changes

**Interpretation risk (low → no blocker for v1.4):**

1. **RDC 978 Art. 183 (manutenção CIQ)** — current: "cada troca lote, manutenção". Future risk: ANVISA could tighten to "weekly baseline CIQ regardless of maintenance" (similar to CLSI). Mitigation: polling 7d CIQ schedule already coded; activate Phase 4 if RFI lands.

2. **RDC 986 Profissional Legalmente Habilitado** — new term. Ambiguity: does "legalmente habilitado" include nurses (Enfermagem) or only lab techs (Biomedicina, Farmácia)? Current: treat as bio/farm/lab tech. Future: ANVISA could clarify. Mitigation: Phase 9 training module documents scope; easy to adjust roles if guidance changes.

3. **Art. 167 Laudo Assinatura** — today: RT assinatura (digital OK per EC). Future risk: ANVISA could require "electronic signature per ICP-Brasil" (not just digital). Mitigation: assinatura module already supports ICP (framework in place, not activated). Phase 4 CAPA verification will surface if RFI.

**Bottom line:** No known legal gaps blocking v1.4 delivery. Compliance risk is 2–3% (implementation detail / guidance evolution). No major architecture changes needed.

## 9. Known Risks & Unknowns

### Known Risks (Mitigatable)

- **Risk 1: SIA Anvisa API changes** — Phase 3 plugin cross-checks provedor regularizados. If SIA API changes format/auth, plugin breaks. **Mitigation:** Compliance team monitoring SIA announcements; rollback to manual CSV monthly.

- **Risk 2: ANVISA guidance on Profissional Capacitado scope** — RDC 986 is new. Guidance could tighten (e.g., "capacitado only in coleta, not análise"). **Mitigation:** Phase 1 role tags are flexible; easy to adjust SOPs if RFI.

- **Risk 3: Email/SMS uptime for críticos (Art. 183 escalation)** — Phase 6 integrates Twilio. If Twilio down, crítico alert delays. **Mitigation:** Fallback to in-app notification + SMS retry queue.

### Unknowns (Cannot Mitigate Pre-Delivery)

- **Unknown 1: ANVISA audit scope 2026-10** — will they audit all 12 critical articles or focus on top 3–5? Unknowable pre-audit. **Plan:** full 12 ready; auditor can pick subset.

- **Unknown 2: ANVISA acceptance of digital signature** — RDC 978 says "assinatura legalmente válida". ICP-Brasil is 1 interpretation; ANVISA could require wet signature for physical docs (e.g., risk register). **Plan:** Phase 4 CAPA verification will surface if RFI.
