================================================================================

1. AVAILABILITY: 99.5%

   - Alert: <99.65% (70% budget consumed)

   - Budget: 1.5% of requests can exceed 3s
   - Alert: YELLOW 2.5s / RED 3.0s sustained

3. ERROR RATE: <0.1%
   - Budget: ~50k errors/month in 5M requests
   - Alert: >0.1% sustained 5 min

4. COMPLIANCE: 100% Audit Trail
   - Budget: Zero missed events (zero tolerance)
   - Alert: <100% capture (RDC 978 violation)
