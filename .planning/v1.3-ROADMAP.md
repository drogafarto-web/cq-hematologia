## Phase 8: CAPA Closure (Stream A — Sequential)

**Goal:** All 12 audit findings closed with 100% evidence and auditor sign-off.  
**Period:** 2026-05-06 → 2026-08-05 (13 weeks — aggressive schedule)  
**Team:** CTO (lead) + Operations + Quality Manager

### Timeline Breakdown

| Week | Tasks | Owner | Deliverable |
|------|-------|-------|--|
| **1–2** (5/6–5/19) | Contact auditor, initiate RFI process for 2 Critical CAPAs | CTO + Ops | RFI letter, evidence gathering starts |
| **3–4** (5/20–6/2) | Submit evidence for Critical CAPAs (2), start High CAPAs (6) | CTO + Ops | Response to auditor, 2 CAPAs closed (target) |
| **5–6** (6/3–6/16) | Submit evidence for High CAPAs (4), start Medium (3) | Team | 4 High CAPAs closed (target) |
| **7–8** (6/17–6/30) | Submit evidence for High CAPAs (2), complete Medium | Team | 6 High + 3 Medium CAPAs closed |
| **9–13** (7/1–8/5) | Extended CAPA (1), auditor final review + sign-off | CTO | All 12 CAPAs signed off, closeout report |

### Deliverables

- ✅ 12 CAPA status records in Firestore (complete with evidence links)
- ✅ Evidence artifacts in Cloud Storage (photos, certificates, policies) — immutable, chain-hashed
- ✅ Auditor RFI responses (documented, timestamped)
- ✅ Closeout report (baseline before/after, % improvement, signed by RT + Auditor)
- ✅ Archive: All CAPA evidence retained 5 years (RDC 978 5.6)

1. **Stream A (Sequential, 1 team):** Phase 8 — CAPA Closure (Weeks 1–13)

**Agent Allocation:**
- **Agent A:** Phase 8 (CAPA) — CTO lead + Ops support
- **Agent B:** Phase 9 (Bioquímica) — Eng A + Eng B
- **Agent C:** Phase 10 (Liberação) — Eng C + Eng D
- **Agent D:** Phases 11–12 (Críticos + Reclamações) — Eng E/F + Eng G/H (staggered)

**Synchronization Points:**
- Weekly standup: Monday 10 AM (all agents)
- Phase boundaries: Each Friday at 5 PM (handoff)
- Integration gate: Phase 10 + 11 merge (mid-July)

## Risk & Contingency

### Risks

| Risk | Likelihood | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|-----------|--------|-----------|
| Auditor unavailable for RFI (Phase 8) | Medium | 2-week delay | Schedule calls weekly; async email |
| Twilio SMS integration fails (Phase 11) | Low | Fallback to email | Email tested in 11-04 |
| Parallel agent conflict (shared services) | Low | Merge conflicts | Branch isolation, code review gate |
| External audit date moves (delay) | Low | 2-week slip | Auditor calendar lock by Week 8 |

### Contingency Plans

- **CAPA closure delays:** Extend Phase 8 by 2 weeks (new deadline 2026-08-19), external audit 2026-09-31
- **Module delays:** De-prioritize Reclamações (12), ship Bio + Lib + Críticos by 2026-08-20, Reclamações as Phase 12.5 (post-audit)
- **Agent unavailability:** Pair programming with senior engineer, no timeline impact if <1 week
