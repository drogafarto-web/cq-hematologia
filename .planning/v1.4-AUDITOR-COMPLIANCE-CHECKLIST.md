10. Risk status (top-10 NPR, new risks, closed risks)
11. Resource adequacy (staffing, budget, tools)
12. Communication effectiveness (internal, external channels)

14. Legal/compliance updates (LGPD, RDC, DICQ changes)

**Auditor verification (annual):**

- Verify meeting held within last 12 months

- Confirm follow-up action items tracked
- Spot-check 3 action items for closure
