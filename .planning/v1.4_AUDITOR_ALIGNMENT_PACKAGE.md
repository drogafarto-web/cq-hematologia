#### Part 3: RFI Cadence Agreement (20 min)
**Speaker:** QA Lead + CTO

- Async RFI submission channel: shared SGD folder + email notification
- **Response SLA: 5 business days** (Mondays submit, Fridays respond)
- Escalation path: if >5 days, CTO direct call within 24h
- RFI template fields:
  - Requirement ID
  - Evidence requested
  - Decision needed (binary: approve/revise)
  - Deadline (for dependency gating)
- Batch frequency: weekly RFI digest (consolidate Mon–Fri submissions)
- Phase 4 weekly reviews (Fridays, 30 min Zoom):
  - CAPA evidence submission review
  - Auditor sign-off or "needs revision" flagged
  - Escalation if critical CAPA >2 iterations

**Output:** Written RFI protocol signed by auditor (email confirmation acceptable)

#### Part 4: Evidence Standards (15 min)
**Speaker:** CTO

- **Digital signature:** Is LogicalSignature (SHA-256 hash + operatorId + timestamp) acceptable as digital signature equivalent for DICQ 4.4 / RDC 978?
  - Current: client-generated, server-validated; no PKI cert
  - Alternative: Cloud HSM signing (cost + complexity)
  - Our position: acceptable for v1.4; PKI v2.0
  - **Auditor input:** approve, require modifications, or escalate to legal review?
- **Tamper evidence:** Is chainHash sufficient, or need external notarization?
  - Current: Firestore immutability + event-chained hashing + baseline reset disclosure
  - Alternative: external timestamp authority (3rd-party notary)
  - Our position: sufficient for lab context; notary optional v1.5
  - **Auditor input:** approve or require external notary?
- **Document retention:** Firestore native + cold-archive (Cloud Storage, 5 years) OR different preference?
  - Current: scheduled daily Firestore backups + quarterly cold-archive export
  - RTO: <4 hours data loss, <24 hours full recovery
  - **Auditor input:** acceptable or alternative requirement?
- **PDF report layout:** Will follow Labclin FR-* forms (physical layout already in production)
  - Includes RT signature block + LogicalSignature QR code (optional enhancement)
  - **Auditor input:** any modifications for audit trail?

**Output:** Evidence standards confirmed (no post-Phase-2 rework) OR adjustment plan filed

- Confirm next checkpoint (end Wave 1, Week 3)

### Risk: Auditor Rejects LogicalSignature Format
**Mitigation:** Pre-alignment call Q&A included (Part 7 of briefing)
- Present legal framework (RDC 978 Art. 115 + RDC 786 Art. 21)
- Show alternative cost (PKI cert: $5k–15k/year)

- If auditor >5 days delayed: CTO direct call + replan Phase 4 timeline
