### 2. PHASE_9_GOVERNANCE_CHECKLIST.json

**Structure:**

- `metadata` — Phase, frameworks (DICQ/RDC/ISO), alert thresholds
- `summary` — Completion %, status, next review date
- `governance_structure` — QD, MR chair, audit coordinator, document controller (auto-populate)
- `categories.A_GOVERNANCE` — 7 items (legal, QD, policies, org chart, MR)
- `categories.D_QUALITY_COMPLIANCE` — 10 items (audit, risk, NC, CAPA, suppliers, equipment)
- `categories.E_PRE_ANALYTICAL` — 5 items (collection, transport, reception, ID, sites)
- `categories.ADDITIONAL_GOVERNANCE` — 10 cross-cutting items (training, POPs, DR, LGPD, manual, audit, regulatory, complaints, CEQ, biosecurity)
- `alert_rules` — Overdue (>30 days), at-risk (7 days), completion lag
- `export_formats` — PDF report, Excel tracker, MR input format
- `integration_points` — Links to educacao-continuada, sgd, auditoria, capa, kpis, labSettings, risks modules
- `success_criteria` — Phase 9 gate + audit readiness + governance maturity

**Data Type:** Machine-readable JSON; can be imported into Excel, Airtable, Monday.com, or custom dashboard

## ❓ Quick FAQs

**Q: How long will Phase 9 take?**  
A: 6–8 weeks for a lab starting from scratch; 3–4 weeks if you already have basic QMS in place.

**Q: What if we don't finish by 2026-08-31?**  
A: Prioritize the 18 **critical** items (marked in JSON). If those are ≥80% complete, you'll pass the Phase 9 gate.

**Q: Can we edit the template?**  
A: Yes. The template is a starting point. Add lab-specific details (governance, risk, suppliers, dates). Don't remove DICQ-required sections.

**Q: Who should approve the Quality Manual?**  
A: Quality Director (primary). Lab Manager or Executive Board may also sign (co-approval).

**Q: What if we don't have an Internal Audit Coordinator?**  
A: Designate a senior staff member (e.g., Quality Coordinator, Department Head). Arrange ISO 19011 training.

**Q: How do we track progress?**  
A: Use the JSON checklist. Update monthly (before MR meeting). Export to PDF for leadership reporting.

**Q: What happens after Phase 9?**  
A: Phase 10 expands the Quality Manual with additional DICQ sections (analytical, post-analytical, resources). Phase 11 is external audit readiness. Phase 12 is regulatory submission.
